Emil Axelsson

I’m a functional programmer specialized in Haskell. I’m particularly interested in embedded domain-specific languages (EDSL) and generic programming.

Research

My research at Chalmers University of Technology resulted in several packages, most notably:

  • RAW-Feldspar
    • An EDSL for high-performance numeric computations and embedded system programming.
  • imperative-edsl
    • A generic package for implementing imperative EDSLs with code generation
  • Syntactic
    • A generic package for representing and manipulating pure expressions in EDSLs
